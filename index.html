About [CV]

I am currently a second-year PhD student at School of Computing, University of Utah, advised by Prof. Qingyao Ai.

I mainly work on search and recommendation using textual knowledge, and explainable IR.

I am also interested in research topics including general sense search and recommendation, graph-centric IR, and Natural Language Understanding.
